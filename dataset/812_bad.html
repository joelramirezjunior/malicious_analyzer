    HOW LONG WILL MY DELIVERY TAKE AFTER PAYMENT IS CONFIRMED


    The delivery 24 hours to arrive at your door step after your payment is confirmed by the accounting department.

    CAN I GET MY DELIVERY ANY WHERE IN THE COUNTRY


    Yes you can. We do deliveries to all 9 provinces at a very cheap cost. We have experienced drivers who handle all deliveries with maximum care. The client also receives a tracking number and an sms alert for all their deliveries.

    WHAT HAPPENS TO BROKEN / CRACKED EGGS AFTER DELIVERY


    Any broken / cracked egg noticed after delivery by our delivery team will be replaced in the next delivery or refund will be made instantly. In case of any broken / cracked eggs a picture or video of themm will be needed for confirmation.
