  • Make

    Record, upload, cut, crop, trim, make GIFs, and more. Not sure where to start? Creating content is easier than ever with our AI script generator and AI-powered editing. And you can always elevate your video by adding interactive, clickable elements.

  • Edit

    Text-based video editing is the easy, fast way to edit videos; our AI-powered editor will even identify "ums" and awkward pauses so you can delete them in a click. You can always trim, crop, and resize your content too. Make your videos shoppable and clickable by adding interactive elements like polls and CTAs. Then, add your logos, font, colors, and watermark to increase brand recognition.

  • Manage

    Keep your content organized in a beautiful video library. And, thanks to auto-transcription, search by caption anytime. Simplify the review process with time-coded notes, frame by frame. Collaborate with specific folks, put your video behind a password, or hide it from everyone but you.

  • Share

    Host your video on our customizable, ad-free player. Embed it on your website, publish it to social, and set permissions so only the right people see it. Then, track your video's performance from one dashboard.
