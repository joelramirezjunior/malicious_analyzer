About Us

TR Global Management Ltd was established in July 2000 as an independent, private, investment management company with offices in İstanbul, Turkey. We have a strong reputation and a proud history, we currently manage $36 billion and employ more than 65 members of staff worldwide. The staff team represents a diverse mix of people from around the world representing more than 20 countries and speaking over 25 languages. Our investment team was founded by bringing together seasoned investment analysts with leaders from the sustainability research field. Sustainable investing for the Long Term's investment approach is based on our conviction that sustainability research integrated into a rigorous traditional investment process can deliver superior investment returns.  Learn More
