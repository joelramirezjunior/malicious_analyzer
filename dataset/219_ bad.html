Who Are We

Welcome to our website, where we specialize in breeding and raising adorable mini Dachshund puppies. With over 17 years of experience, we are dedicated to providing healthy, well-socialized puppies to loving homes across Australia.

01

Socialization is also a key aspect of our breeding program. From an early age, our puppies are exposed to various stimuli, including different environments, sounds, and people. This helps them develop into well-rounded and confident dogs, ready to adapt to their new families and surroundings.

02

We are committed to upholding the highest standards in our breeding practices. Our goal is to produce mini Dachshund puppies that not only meet breed standards but also possess excellent temperament and health. We carefully select our breeding dogs, ensuring they are free from genetic health issues and have sound temperaments.

Health and well-being are our top priorities. All our puppies receive regular veterinary care, including vaccinations, deworming, and thorough health checks. We work closely with our trusted veterinarian to ensure that our puppies are in optimal health before they go to their new homes.
