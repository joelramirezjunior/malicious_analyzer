About us

Locate your unique family companion.

Each Japanese Spitz puppy is ANKC / UKC registered with certified pedigrees and champion bloodlines. Each of our  snuggly puppy that leaves our home is dewormed, vaccinated, microchipped and receives a thorough wellness-exam by our local VET doctor. We always provide them their first teeth brushing experience as well. We do frequent grooming of each puppy and  hand-socialize them to ensure a smooth transition for them once they leave for their forever homes. Our pups generally travel very well and sleep through the first night which makes it better for them and you! We include a Pet Parent Puppy Bag for you, to cover all the essentials of welcoming your puppy home.
We try to ensure our puppies go to only the best homes where they will continue to be loved and cared for. We appreciate your patience as we screen each inquiry / applicant.
Kindly feel free to surf around our website and enjoy our quality content which we constantly update and kindly  ask any questions if you have any doubts or require clarification.
