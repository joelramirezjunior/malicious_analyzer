Our Services

Packaging & Storage

Packaging & Storage of all shipments is done at our warehouse before the shipment is confirmed and sent out for delivery.

Warehousing

All paid consignments are kept at aby of our warehouses after successful pickup from the sender pending dispatch to its final destination.

Cargo Security

Each shipment is executed individually at SQR TRANSPORTATION completely according to your requirements and packages arrive in time and reliably.

Door-to-door Delivery

Door-to-door delivery is possible with us depending on the client's delivery address. Please contact our team for more details on this mode of delivery.

Real time tracking

We also offer real time tracking updates for every location where the shipment momentarily stops, the client is able to visually track the progress of the package till it arrives at its destination.

24/7 Customer Service

We have dedicated and patient customer service agents working in shifts to enure that all client enquiries can be handled in the shortest possible tiime.
