About Us

Welcome to Moe's Pup Family

Best Homebred Exotic Puppies

We own a family business, breeders of exotic puppies. We're giving out healthy, well trained, purebreed and certified toy puppies for deserving homes. Our puppies are vet checked and certified. All these puppies are born and raised in our home. We have a passion for toy puppies and we derive great pleasure in filling your homes with these lovely creatures.
When you purchase a puppy from us, delivery is done within 48 hours to your homes. No waiting lists, we have a first come, first served policy and we only list puppies when they are ready for their new homes. Our return and refund policy is simple and straight forward. All displayed photos are most recent and we take down puppies as they get selected by buyers.
We raise our puppies in a family environment, they're friendly and accustomed to kids, strangers and other animals. They have been potty trained and are up to date on all their shots. We have the perfect puppies for your family. Don't hesitate to contact us if you fall in love with one of our beauties.

Our Breeding Philosophy

Why Choose Us

Moe's Pup Family is recognized as a breeder of some of the finest Mini bernedoodles and cavoodle puppies in the country for over 15 years. If you're looking for a quality bred puppy, we can meet and exceed your needs and provide great personal support along the way. When you acquire your new puppy from Moe's Pup Family you are guaranteed that he / she has been given the best nutrition, care, imprinting, and training possible.

The Dog

At Moe's Pup Family, we are committed to breeding puppies with unmatched quality in all aspects:
Anatomy
Health
Intelligence
Beauty
and above all TEMPERAMENT!!
We insist on developing the highest levels in each aspect as a part of a dog breeding philosophy that we refer to as “The Total Dog”. This philosophy has been the cornerstone of our efforts from the very beginning. If you want to truly see and understand the difference, bring your whole family along to visit. Once you have had the chance to be in the presence of and interact with our dogs, you will see the unsurpassed quality and temperament of our puppies! Our dogs are all part of our extended family and are treated as such.

The Experience
From your first contact, you will see a level of personal service that you will truly appreciate and should expect. We put great emphasis on developing and establishing relationships that will meet the needs of both our customers and their companions. We can / will offer advice along the way in every aspect of your new relationship. We trust that you will treat your new companion as a friend and family member rather than an object or possession and repay them for the never ending devotion and unconditional loyalty they will gladly give to you each and every day.
