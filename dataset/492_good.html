What's happening in Washington

Public comment period open for 2024-2026 hunting season proposed rule changes

Submit your comments online and by phone or mail until March 25. Comments can also be submitted in person at the public hearing on March 26.

Senator Sam Hunt announces his retirement

The senator plans to retire when his term ends in January 2025. Learn more about his career and read his announcement letter.

Medal of Merit and Medal of Valor awarded for the first time in 9 years

Learn more about our states highest civilian honors and view the award ceremony that took place on February 21.

Currently hiring seasonal park aides for spring and summer

What if your office was a forest, beach, or historic fort? Apply to become a park aide and share your love of the outdoors with others.

Upcoming tolling changes for I-405/SR 167 corridor

Starting March 1, the I-405 express lanes and SR 167 HOV lanes will have increased rates and hours of operation.

Share your input on Washingtons workforce plan

Talent and Prosperity for All is the state workforce development strategy for 2024-2028. Take the survey and submit comments through March 15.

Looking for SecureAccess Washington (SAW)?

SecureAccess Washington is a tool to get access to all online state government services in one place. Use a single login to access services including licensing, employment security, and paid leave.
