Money Back Guarantee

We will gladly take back our pup even after a year or whenever you feel like you don’t need it. We are against puppy mills so you can call us if you're no longer able to keep the dog. All of our pups and dogs are sold with a minimum of 1-year health guarantee against genetic hip, heart & eye defects.

Our dogs are raised not only for the quality of the breed but for their human-friendly/compatibility and unique temperament. All of our dogs are handled by our children and raised in a loving environment. We would be proud to help you find your next companion. We'll be glad to add a new member to your home. We have passionately bred and given out puppies to many homes.

They come with a one year genetic health guarantee.
