The Sphynx cat breed is widely known for their nearly hairless appearance, but these endearing, clever, and affectionate felines have a lot more to offer. Discover what it’s like to live with a Sphynx cat. Disposition
These stunning bald cats are inquisitive, outgoing, highly intelligent, and vocal about their desires – quite literally. Be prepared for plenty of cat conversations as your pet will follow you around from room to room.

Sphynxes are amusing, friendly, and natural entertainers who will do anything to capture your attention (and affection). These sociable and playful cats crave attention and will stay by your side for hours. They love to snuggle under warm blankets with you on chilly mornings or even on warmer days. Their clingy nature may not suit every pet parent, but those who adore the Sphynx breed will receive unparalleled companionship that is hard to find elsewhere. These cats are devoted and affectionate pets that will love you without limits.

According to Blake Gipson, breeder of Bemisu Sphynx, “[Sphynxes are] part dog, part cat, part monkey, and part human. They are highly adaptable and intelligent. They are an interactive breed and express curiosity towards all living things.”

Do not be surprised if your Sphynx cat finds his way into any room, cupboard, or cabinet in the house. These cats are just as curious as the proverb suggests and are highly agile, using dexterous finger-like toes to poke, prod, and open doors. Consider doing some light cat-proofing before bringing a Sphynx kitten home!
