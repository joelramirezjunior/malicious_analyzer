Cavapoo Varieties

  • F1 Cavapoos: First Generation (F1) Cavapoos are the resulting of crossing a purebred Cavalier King Charles Spaniel with a purebred mini Poodle or toy Poodle. These F1 Cavapoos are 50% Cavalier and 50% poodle.
  • F1b Cavapoos: First Generation Backcross (F1b) Cavapoos take a First Generation (F1) Cavapoo (mentioned above) and cross it back to a mini Poodle or Toy Poodle. We have occasional F1b litters primarily for those with severe allergies, who want to be sure that their Cavapoo puppy has a non-shedding poodle coat. F1b Cavapoos are 25% Cavalier King Charles Spaniel and 75% Poodle.
  • Multigenerational Cavapoos: Multigenerational Cavapoos (multigens for short) are Cavapoo puppies where both the mother and the father are Cavapoos. You can breed to F1 Cavapoos to each other (non-related 0f course) and get Multigen Cavapoo pups, sometimes referred to as F2 Cavapoos. Or you can breed two F1b Cavapoos to each other and get F2b Cavapoo puppies.

WHY A CAVAPOO

Cavapoos are one of todays top designer dog breeds due to their forever puppy look and their kind temperament.

Cavapoos have round faces, a black button nose, floppy ears and large round eyes. They can be a variety of colors.  

Like the Cavalier they love to please and tend to be affectionate.  Their sensitive personalities tend to make good therapy dogs.

Because Cavapoos are half poodle they are low shedders.  This makes them them attractive to those who have allergies.

Cavapoos have a long life expectancy. Usually ranging between 11-13  years. The weight of our puppies will probably be between 13-18 lbs. Making them a good choice for apartment living.

They tend to be highly intelligent making them quick learners and easy to train. Obedience training and tricks are usually easy for them to learn.
