Our Story

At Armani Puppies Home, we want to help you raise your puppy to have a happy and healthy life at their new forever home! That’s why we offer premium puppy benefits with all of our puppies. We offer a lifetime microchip membership and puppy training with the ANKC’s My Pet Trainer program.

Our Products

Our pet training will help you be successful in raising your new family companion! Our program includes everything you need from registering your puppy’s microchip, online and phone support, online and over the phone puppy training, and so much more!

Our Services

At Armani Puppies Home, we offer a variety of services to help keep your pets happy and healthy, including grooming, training, and veterinary care. Our experienced staff and state-of-the-art facilities ensure that your pets receive the best care possible.
