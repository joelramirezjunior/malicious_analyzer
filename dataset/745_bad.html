A Bit About Us…

We are a husband and wife team who love all animals and especially Ragdoll cats. We have a small hobby cattery in our home. We are both retired, so we have lots of time to care for our precious Ragdolls. We have three adult  females (queens) and one adult  male (king), all selected by us for their sweet personalities, vigorous health, and stunning beauty. All of our cats have tested negative for feline leukemia virus (FeLV), feline immunodeficiency virus (FIV), and feline coronavirus which causes feline infectious peritonitis (FIP). In addition, our cats have all tested negative for feline hypertrophic cardiomyopathy (HCM), a disease of the heart muscle in which the walls thicken and the heart enlarges. Please feel free to contact us if you are interested in purchasing a Ragdoll cat from us.
