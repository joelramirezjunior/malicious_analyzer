Welcome To Hugo Pets Home

 

Finding the perfect pet doesn’t have to be full of mystery or compromise. The Hugo Pets Home Promise means you can find the right puppy/kitten with confidence and ease.

 

Superb quality is found very near to where the joy and passion of one’s heart lies. At our home  our passion simply brings out the best puppies/kittens, We embrace the entire embodiment of the breeds: the compact , the family friendly personality, and the loving companionship. This passion and quality for excellence is seen through our   Puppies/kittens  for sale.  Puppies/kittens  produced from our kennel are closely tied to our core breeding values of health, body, temperament, and companionship. These core values are fundamental in producing the high quality   Puppies/kittens that we are known for. We are Small Pet Home breeders.

Our mission is to provide happy, healthy, well socialized puppies/kitten. While accomplishing this mission, it is extremely important to us to provide a clean, fun, safe, and comfortable home for our parent dogs/cats. We prefer the designer breeds over purebreds because we believe they are healthier, easier to train, and make better companions/members of your family.

We have a lot of experience with puppies and kittens. We also have an assistant who comes in to help bath and play with the puppies and kittens for 4 or more hours a day. She is a great help in taking care  and giving them the extra attention and love when they are at our house. She is well trained in taking care of pets  Our puppies/kittens are usually shipped with a nanny on board. They do not fly alone. They are flown with us in the cabin. We are there to hand your special baby to you. 

Our puppies/kittens are first and foremost family companions.  Second they can serve as show dogs, hunting companions, therapy dogs, service and/or working dogs/cats. No matter if you are looking for a loving member to add to your family or are interested in a certain dog sport or venue, our puppies/kittens excel in all areas. We have hand selected each parent for the best health and genetics with an awesome pedigree. Our puppies/kittens are healthy, vet checked and come with a health guarantee for one year. 

We have some of the best puppies/kittens you will find anywhere. We enjoy matching each family with the perfect puppy/kitten that will bring many years of fun and companionship.

All of our puppies and kittens will come with a one-year health guarantee, up to date on vaccinations (medical records provided), and micro chipped. Also included is a starter kit with: a free toy and blanket, puppy food, instruction/care documents, vet examination papers, and microchip information. Before going to their new home, our puppies/kittens will have a veterinary examination including, but not limited to: coat & skin, eyes, ears, nose & throat, mouth, teeth, gums, legs & paws, heart, abdomen, lungs, and the gastrointestinal & urogenital systems.
