The biggest online dictionary

Glosbe is the biggest community-built dictionary. It supports ALL languages in the world! Join us today!

Glosbe is a platform providing free dictionaries with in-context translations (translated sentences - the so-called translation memory). You will find here:

  • billions of translated phrases
  • phrase illustrations
  • recordings and pronunciation
  • billions of translated sentences
  • automatic translator of longer texts

Glosbe is developed thanks to the efforts of many people who add new content every day. Join us and share your knowledge!
