Welcome to Neutral Puppies Home

Neutral Puppies Home is a family-owned and operated business that has been breeding and raising puppies for over a decade. Located in the heart of South Africa, our kennel is dedicated to producing healthy, happy, and well-socialized puppies that will make great additions to any family. Our journey began when we fell in love with the unique personalities and characteristics of these puppies. 


We were fascinated by their playful and curious nature, as well as their affectionate and loving personalities. We started our breeding program with a focus on health, temperament, and quality, and we have continued to refine our breeding practices over the years to ensure that our puppies are the best that they can be. 


At Neutral Puppies Home , we take our responsibility to our puppies and their future families very seriously. We believe that a happy and healthy puppy is one that is raised with love, care, and attention. That’s why we provide our puppies with the best possible care from the moment they are born.
