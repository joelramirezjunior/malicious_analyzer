Welcome

Ems Inspection Solutions Ltd was formed in 2014, as an International Shipping Company based in England that aims to deal with both the intricate logistics and freight forwarding requirements of major companies and PLC's as well as smaller companies. With everybody receiving the same high level of professional service - whether using our express ground services, cheaper freight forwarding or groupage freight shipping services.

We offer a range of international shipping services - baggage shipping, personal effects shipping, car shipping, fine art shipping, furniture shipping.

The key to our success is the calibre of our people. You'll find they will go the extra mile to ensure that your international move goes exactly to plan. We work to recognized quality standards guaranteeing that your goods will be handled carefully and professionally, from start to finish. Our shipment tracking enables us to monitor the location and status of your goods, while our administrative team ensures that all shipping documentation is processed quickly and efficiently.

As members of the specialist Overseas Group of the British Association of Removers (BAR) we are regularly assessed and bound by the International Movers Mutual Insurance Company Ltd's advance payment guarantee scheme, for your total protection.

Ems Inspection Solutions Ltd specialises in overseas car shipping. We expertly and securely ship many types of vehicles and heavy machinery from the UK to Europe and the rest of the world.

Ems Inspection Solutions Ltd operates regular import and export road freight and Air Freight services to and from : Belgium, Holland, Luxembourg, Germany, Austria, France, Switzerland, Italy, Greece, Liechtenstein, Spain, Portugal, Gibraltar, Malta, Norway, Denmark, Sweden, Finland, Iceland, Latvia, Czech Republic, Bosnia-Herzegovina, Bulgaria, Croatia, Poland, Hungary, Lithuania, Slovakia, Turkey, Canada and the USA,
