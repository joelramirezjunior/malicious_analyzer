Albeka British Shorthair

The British Shorthair is the pedigreed version of the traditional British domestic cat, with a distinctively stocky body, dense coat, and broad face.

Welcome to Albeka British Shorthair

Our kittens are raised with love and care, ensuring they are well-socialized and ready to become cherished members of your family. Whether you’re seeking a playful companion or a laid-back lap cat, the British Shorthair breed offers a perfect balance of affection and independence. With their gentle temperament and easygoing nature, British Shorthair kittens make wonderful pets for individuals and families alike. Their adaptability and friendly disposition are sure to bring joy and warmth to any household.

🏡 Our Kittens’ Upbringing 🏡

Raised with love and care in a nurturing environment, our British Shorthair kittens receive the best possible start in life. They are well-socialized, vaccinated, and health-checked to ensure they are ready to bring joy and warmth to their new forever homes.

WHY CHOOSE US

We breed for type and temperaments while their health is always the first priority. All of the breeding (or original) cats are DNA tested PKD negative. We ensure all of them are raised in house with love and care and guaranteed to be socialised, healthy and free of genetic faults.

We sell kittens to selected families who are responsible and willing to provide the best needs for their cat’s daily care. Please introduce yourself when making an inquiry and we will be happy to answer any questions you have.
