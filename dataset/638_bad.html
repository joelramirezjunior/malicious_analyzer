Welcome to Holding Eggs Farm.

There are many reasons to choose our eggs, including:

  • Our eggs are collected fresh from the nest every day. We never sell eggs that are more than a few days old.
  • Our eggs are high in protein and other nutrients. They are also free of antibiotics and hormones.
  • Our chickens are raised in a sustainable environment, and we use sustainable farming practices.
  • Our chickens are treated humanely and have access to fresh air, sunlight, and plenty of space to roam.
