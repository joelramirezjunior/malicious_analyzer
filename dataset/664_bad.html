YOU WANT TO BUY A VEHICLE ON THE INTERNET?

Euro-Max Transporte A&J GmbH mission is to be recognized as an industry leader in the vehicle transportation marketplace by providing exceptional service and value to our customers and suppliers. We are committed to our client’s requirements by delivering cost effective, ethical, and reliable transportation solutions by utilizing the latest innovations in technology, while sustaining a challenging and rewarding work environment for our greatest asset, our employees.

WE HAVE OUR OWN VEHICLE INSPECTION DEPARTMENT

Everything we deliver is carefully inspected. We have our own team of qualified inspectors that generates a full report for each vehicle.

THE MOST COMPLETE PACKAGE OF SERVICES

Euro-Max Transporte A&J GmbH will give you an accurate quote based on weight and distance. There will be no hidden charges or surprises. Your shipping quote will not change, nor will any charges be added, unless the information you provide changes or you request additional services. If you know the weight of your shipment, that is perfect. If you are not sure, we can usually estimate the weight of items like furniture and packed boxes based on your description.
