TRANSPORT AND FREIGHT FORWARDING

With strategically located facilities either within or close to major ports, Blenheim Shipping provides highly efficient port centric 3PL solutions for the receiving, handling, storing, picking, packing and distributing of goods around the UK.

In excess of 1.5 million square feet of warehousing is available, including bonded warehouse services, together with 20 acres of open storage, while over 100 vehicles complete over 120,000 container and general haulage moves each year.

Each facility is resourced with the assets and people to meet the requirements of the customers they serve and the cargoes they handle, from retail goods in the FMCG sector to bulk cargoes including forest products and steel.

Blenheim  customers benefit from a truly flexible range of warehousing and distribution options, either as individual services or as an efficient and comprehensive third party logistics (3PL) resource, that can be tailored to answer to the most demanding customer requirements.
